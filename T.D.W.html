Other Details





Debsirin School is an all boys school near Hua Lampong Train Station. Debsirin school offers 4 types of cirliculum which are SMTE EP IEP and normal classes. Each class offer deifferent types of education. With EP offering classes in English and SMTE offering classes mainly focusing on science and mathamethics. IEP is a semi english Program offering less English Classes than EP program.
Debsirin School is known for football. Wether if it is the big football field, the football club, football competitions, Debsirin is known for it all.
